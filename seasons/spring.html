Spring

If this is your first year then you won't be able to make much money off of Spring unless you plant Strawberries, which cost a lot of money for the seeds. Instead, if you're looking for money you should go fishing. While fishing may be boring, it brings in a lot of money in the Spring, but once you make more and more money from crops it becomes less profitable.
