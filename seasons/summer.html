Summer

Summer is one of the most profitable seasons for the farmer. Blueberries are in rotation, which sells for a lot and also produce many from one plant. A lot of the flowers are also available to buy from Pierre's in the summer, so if you're looking to make your farm pretty that's an option to look out for.
