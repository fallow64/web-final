Fall

In Fall, there isn't really much to do. If you have any animals, this is the time that I would be upgrading any of my barns or coops because this is when you have the money. However, don't spend it all, because you still need money to plant seeds in Spring because Winter is a bad season to make any cash. The best crop to grow in Fall is the cranberry.
